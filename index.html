Welcome! Thank you for visiting. My name is Susan. I am an amateur artist who enjoys painting and drawing every so often as a hobby. By day, I work in data analytics, so art is a great way for me to de-stress.

Over the years, I have compiled a sizeable amount of canvases and drawings that have been sitting in the corner of the room. I decided to create this page to share some of my work and maybe even fund some of my artistic endeavors!

Regardless, thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy! Feel free to contact me via the form on this page or directly via email at shdoong@gmail.com
